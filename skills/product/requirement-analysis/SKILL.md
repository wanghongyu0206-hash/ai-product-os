# Requirement Analysis Skill

## Goal

Transform ambiguous requirements into structured and actionable product requirements.

## Workflow

### Step 1

Understand business context.

Questions:

- Why is this needed?
- What business problem is being solved?
- What metrics are affected?

### Step 2

Identify stakeholders.

Examples:

- End User
- Administrator
- Operations Team
- Product Team

### Step 3

Identify user needs.

Separate:

- Explicit needs
- Implicit needs

### Step 4

Extract functional requirements.

Format:

Feature
Description
Priority

### Step 5

Extract non-functional requirements.

Examples:

Performance

Security

Accessibility

Reliability

### Step 6

Identify risks.

Examples:

Technical Risk

Business Risk

Compliance Risk

### Step 7

Generate requirement summary.
