# Chart Skill

## Goal

Choose the appropriate chart type based on the business question rather than visual preference.

## Workflow

### Step 1

Identify the business question.

Examples:

- Trend
- Comparison
- Distribution
- Composition
- Ranking

### Step 2

Choose the chart type.

Trend

- Line Chart

Comparison

- Bar Chart

Distribution

- Histogram

Composition

- Pie Chart
- Donut Chart

Ranking

- Horizontal Bar Chart

### Step 3

Keep charts simple.

Rules:

- One chart answers one question.
- Avoid excessive legends.
- Minimize visual clutter.

### Step 4

Provide context.

Display:

- Title
- Time Range
- Unit
- Data Source (optional)

### Step 5

Support interaction.

Examples:

Tooltip

Legend Toggle

Date Range Switch

Export

### Step 6

Generate chart specification.
