# Business Model Skill

## Goal

Design a clear and sustainable business model.

## Workflow

### Step 1

Identify customer segments.

Examples:

- Enterprise
- SMB
- Individual Users

### Step 2

Define value proposition.

Questions:

- What problem is solved?
- Why is the solution better?
- What value is created?

### Step 3

Determine revenue model.

Examples:

- Subscription
- Freemium
- Pay Per Use
- License
- Advertising

### Step 4

Identify key resources.

Examples:

- Technology
- Team
- Data
- Brand

### Step 5

Identify key costs.

Examples:

- Development
- Cloud Services
- Marketing
- Customer Support

### Step 6

Evaluate sustainability.

Output a complete business model summary.
