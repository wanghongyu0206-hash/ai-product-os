# Design Review Skill

## Goal

Review the design from the perspectives of users, business, and implementation.

## Workflow

### Step 1

Review business goals.

Questions:

- Does the design support the business objective?
- Are key features covered?

### Step 2

Review user experience.

Check:

- User task completion
- Navigation clarity
- Interaction consistency

### Step 3

Review visual structure.

Check:

- Layout hierarchy
- Information density
- Component consistency

### Step 4

Review implementation feasibility.

Check:

- Can the design be implemented efficiently?
- Are reusable components used?

### Step 5

Identify issues.

Categorize by priority:

- Critical
- High
- Medium
- Low

### Step 6

Generate improvement suggestions.

Output a structured review report.
