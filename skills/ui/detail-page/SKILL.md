# Detail Page Skill

## Goal

Design detail pages that balance information display with operational efficiency.

## Workflow

### Step 1

Identify the business object.

Examples:

- Conversation
- Customer
- Knowledge Document
- Agent
- Project

### Step 2

Identify user goals.

Examples:

- View details
- Edit
- Audit
- Export
- Delete

### Step 3

Group information.

Recommended sections:

Basic Information

Business Information

History

Related Records

Attachments

### Step 4

Design page layout.

Recommended order:

Header

↓

Summary Card

↓

Main Information

↓

Related Information

↓

Activity Timeline

### Step 5

Design actions.

Examples:

Edit

Delete

Export

Share

Archive

### Step 6

Output detail page specification.
