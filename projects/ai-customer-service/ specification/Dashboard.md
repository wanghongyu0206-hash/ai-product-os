# Dashboard Specification

## Page Goal

Provide a real-time overview of AI customer service operations and enable users to quickly identify key business metrics, trends, and pending tasks.

---

## Target Users

- Operations Manager
- Customer Service Supervisor
- Product Manager

---

## Business Objectives

Users should be able to:

- Monitor conversation volume
- Monitor AI resolution performance
- Monitor customer satisfaction
- Identify unresolved conversations
- Access frequently used actions
