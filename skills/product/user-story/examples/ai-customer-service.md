# AI Customer Service

## Story 1

As a customer,

I want to ask questions through AI chat,

So that I can receive answers immediately.

Acceptance Criteria

- AI responds within 3 seconds.
- Conversation history is saved.

---

## Story 2

As an administrator,

I want to manage the knowledge base,

So that AI provides accurate answers.

Acceptance Criteria

- Support document upload.
- Support document deletion.
- Support version management.
